MAIN PROJECT

This application is about creating a shelf for your favorite books. It is your own personal library.

Using Google Books API's, the most relevant books for your search will appear in the following table (surrounded with red borders). You may choose the book that you want and store it in the Database (add it to the bookshelf surrounded with green borders).

Notice: This search presents only the first 10 books relevant to your search. It is based on the book's name. You can also only store 10 books in the bookshelf. Further enhancements are planned (all the relevant books, search based on the author, year of publishing...).
